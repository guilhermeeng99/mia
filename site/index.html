Dictate anywhere, entirely on-device

The whole pipeline — capture, voice detection, transcription, cleanup, injection — runs natively in Rust on your machine.

Push-to-talk, anywhere

A global hotkey that works even when MIA is unfocused. Hold, speak, release — text appears in the app you're using.

Available

Faithful transcription

Whisper (whisper.cpp), kept warm in memory for low latency. pt-BR and English are first-class; ~99 languages supported.

Available

Anti-hallucination by design

Silero VAD gating, greedy decoding, and stateless per-utterance requests. Only your actual speech reaches the decoder.

Available

Polished text cleanup

Deterministic rules strip fillers (um/uh/é/tipo), apply spoken punctuation ("nova linha", "vírgula"), collapse stutters, and fix casing.

Available

Types at your cursor

System-wide injection via Windows SendInput (Unicode keystrokes), with a clipboard + Ctrl+V fallback for long text.

Available

Floating mic HUD & tray

An always-on-top, click-through HUD shows listening → transcribing → inserting with a live waveform. Close to the system tray.

Available

Optional GPU acceleration

An optional NVIDIA CUDA engine (~7–10× faster), downloaded on demand. Falls back to a bundled CPU build.

Available

Dictionary & snippets

Personal vocabulary and word replacement, plus voice-triggered text expansion for phrases you say all the time.

In progress

Signed auto-update

Updates delivered in-app and verified with minisign, so you always run a build the author actually signed.

Three steps, then it just types

1

Hold the hotkey

Press and hold your push-to-talk key — it works in any app, even when MIA isn't focused.

2

Speak

Say what you want written. The floating HUD shows MIA listening and transcribing in real time.

3

Release

MIA cleans up the text and types it at your cursor — polished, faithful, no creative rewriting.

Why local

Private

Capture, voice detection, transcription, cleanup and injection all run on your machine. Your voice never leaves the device — no telemetry, no server.

Fast

A warm, resident Whisper model and a native Rust engine keep latency low. Optional NVIDIA CUDA makes it ~7–10× faster.

Faithful

Deterministic cleanup and anti-hallucination guards are always on. MIA is a faithful dictation tool, not a creative rewriter.
